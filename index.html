I am a Masters student at McGill University advised by Prof. Hsiu-Chin Lin. My research focuses on application of Machine Learning in the control of Robots for manipulation and locomotion. Specifically, I work on improving accuracy of dynamic control in manipulator robots by learning their inertial parameters from trajectory data. I am excited about applying my research to build fully autonomous systems.
